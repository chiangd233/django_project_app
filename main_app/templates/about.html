About

This application was created by me, Dominick Chiang, on April 16th, 2022 to encourage everyone to live a healthier lifestyle and get more into exercising. Living the 9-5 lifestyle and with COVID going on, I was becoming very stationary sitting at a desk from 9-5 for 5 days a week. I reached the heaviest weight in December of 2021 at 235 lbs. At this point, I was determined to live a healthier lifestyle and find more time to exercise.

This application was made to share and create different kinds of workouts using a exercise database. Exercises can be reused to create new workouts. If you don't see a workout, you can add one!

If there are any issues with this application, please email chiangd233@gmail.com.
